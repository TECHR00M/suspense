Supply and Demand of Stock Price of Company Depends on:

1) Company Fundamentals

  • Earning of company, loss of company , future growth of company
  • Management and Governance Team
  • Expansion Plans
  • Debt Position (Negative Impacts)

People invest by looking fundamental.

 

2) Technical Factors

  • Liquidity of Shares
  • Positive and Negative news 
  • Charts and Patterns (Moving Average ...,)
  • Court Cases
  • Company Annual Report
  • Social Media
  • Quarterly Earning Reports

3) Macro and Micro Economics

  • Social and Political Stability( Because government creates rules for business to run, if it is good then grow) eg. Infrastructure Company, Hydropower Company which depends on government policies
  • Nepal and World Economy
  • Inflation

4) Human Sentiment

  • Users reacts to shares and grow exponentially like 1 to 2, 2 to 4 and soon.
  • Statistics say forecast the future on the basis of past.
  • The chart gives past Trend
  • The price of the product depends on: Buyers' and Sellers Bidding.
  • No of Buyers Bids: at Price a, at price b, at price c ....: Select Best Buyer
  • No. of Seller Bids: at Price a, at price b, at price c, .....: Select Best Seller
  • So, Best Seller and Best Buyer determine the buy and sell price of the product.
  • But this price of the product (say share) fluctuates rapidly every second.
