Blue Chip Stock (source of update)

  • Share of a company that has been making profits for a long time and paying high dividends to investors are called blue-chip shares.
  • Such companies are good for investors.
  • These companies win the trust of investors through transparency, clear division of responsibilities, equitable distribution of returns etc.
  • The market value of shares of such companies is often rising.

Income Stock

  • This stock gives a good Cash return to the shareholder each year.
  • They generally give good interest as compared to the interest given by the bank.

Growth Stock

  • Growth stocks are those companies expected to grow sales and earnings at a faster rate than the market average
  • Can be little risk than above two.

Seasonal Stock

  • There are certain stocks that have different demands at different times of the year and are affected by the seasonal shifts to a great extent. 
  • To understand this better let us consider the current season i.e. summers. We all know that during summers demand for ACs, coolers, refrigerators, hotels increase.
  • Consider another example, during Diwali demand for Jewelry, mobile phones, laptops, and other luxury items increase. So, an investor can consider investing in such stocks few months before when the price is low and then sell off during October or November.
  • Here an investor can optimally use the historical data that gives an indication of the past trends.
  •  BUT do not depend solely on seasonality factor, consider other fundamental factors as well before making an investment.

Defense Stock

A Defensive Stock is a stock that provides steady growth and earnings to the investors in the form of dividends irrespective of the state of the economy as it has a low correlation with the overall stock market/economy and is therefore insulated from changing business cycles. 

Examples of Defensive sector stocks include utilities, consumer durables, pharmaceuticals or medical stocks, and real estate.
