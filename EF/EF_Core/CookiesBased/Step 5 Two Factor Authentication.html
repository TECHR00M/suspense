Multi-factor Authentication (MFA) is an authentication method that requires the user to provide two or more verification factors to gain access to a resource like username/password, mobile phone , finger prints , facial attributes.

Two Factor Authentication(TFA) is a authentication where you use 2 verification factors 1) Username and Password and 2) Mobile Phone (sending random code via SMS)

or Username/Password and Email .

The two-step verification is a process where a user enters credentials, and after successful password validation,  receives an OTP (one-time-password) via email or SMS. Then, they enter that OTP in the Two-Step Verification form on our site to log in successfully.

For two factor authentication we need authenticator apps.Common are Microsoft Authenticator and Google Authenticator Apps
